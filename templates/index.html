How FaceShape Works And What It Does ?

FaceShape detects your face with computer vision technologies and then determines the shape of your face with machine learning techniques, thus allowing you to find the most suitable glasses and hair style for you. It is sufficient to see your face clearly in the photo you take for the system to work.
